Ply3rs — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026  |  Effective date: March 30, 2026

Summary: Ply3rs does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device. This policy complies with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and Google Play's Data Safety requirements.

1. Data Controller

The data controller for this application is:

Ply3rs
Email: ply3rs.app@gmail.com

As the data controller, we are responsible for ensuring your data is handled in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including the GDPR (EU) 2016/679.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Ply3rs is a fully offline media player. The app does not include any analytics, tracking, advertising SDKs, or telemetry of any kind. No data is ever sent to us or any third party.

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Ply3rs stores the following data locally on your device only, for the sole purpose of providing the app's functionality:

  • Playlist data — IPTV playlist URLs and channel information you add
  • Login credentials — Xtream Codes or network drive credentials you enter (stored encrypted using AES-256 encryption)
  • Favorites — Channels you mark as favorites
  • Playback position — Resume position for media you've watched
  • Language preference — Your chosen app language
GDPR Legal Basis: The processing of locally stored data is based on Article 6(1)(b) GDPR — processing necessary for the performance of the contract (providing the app's functionality to you). No consent is required as no data leaves your device.

4. Data Transmission

Ply3rs connects to the internet only when you explicitly request it, to:

  • Fetch IPTV playlists from URLs you provide
  • Stream media content from sources you configure
  • Connect to network drives (SMB, NFS, FTP) you set up

The app does not contact any server owned or operated by us. All network connections are initiated solely by you and go directly to the content sources you specify. No personal data is transmitted.

5. Third-Party Services

None. Ply3rs does not integrate any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, crash reporting tools, or social media SDKs. There are no third-party data processors.

6. Data Sharing & International Transfers

We do not share any data with anyone. Since we do not collect or transmit data, there is nothing to share, sell, or transfer to third parties. There are no international data transfers, as all data remains on your device.

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights under GDPR. Since all data is stored locally on your device and we have no access to it, you can exercise these rights directly:

  • Right of Access (Art. 15) — All your data is on your device; you have full access at all times
  • Right to Rectification (Art. 16) — You can edit your playlists, credentials, and settings at any time within the app
  • Right to Erasure (Art. 17) — You can delete all app data by clearing app data in Android Settings, or by uninstalling the app
  • Right to Data Portability (Art. 20) — Your playlist data is stored in standard formats on your device
  • Right to Restriction of Processing (Art. 18) — You can stop using specific features at any time
  • Right to Object (Art. 21) — Since we perform no data processing beyond local storage, there is no processing to object to

To exercise any of these rights or if you have questions, contact us at ply3rs.app@gmail.com.

8. Your Rights Under CCPA

If you are a California resident, the CCPA grants you the following rights:

  • Right to Know — We do not collect personal information
  • Right to Delete — Uninstall the app or clear app data to delete all locally stored data
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale — We do not sell any personal information
  • Right to Non-Discrimination — We do not discriminate against users exercising their privacy rights

9. Children's Privacy

Ply3rs does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EEA). The app does not collect personal information from any user of any age. The app is not directed at children and complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and GDPR Article 8.

10. Data Security

We implement the following security measures to protect data stored on your device:

  • Encryption — Login credentials are encrypted using AES-256-GCM via Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences
  • No Cloud Backup — App data is excluded from Android cloud backup to prevent unintended data exposure
  • Minimal Permissions — The app requests only Internet and Network State permissions; no access to location, contacts, phone, camera, or microphone
  • Certificate Pinning — Only system-trusted certificates are accepted for network connections
  • Code Obfuscation — Release builds use R8 code shrinking and obfuscation

11. Permissions

Ply3rs requests only the minimum permissions necessary:

  • INTERNET — To stream media from URLs and playlists you provide
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — To detect connectivity status

The app does not request access to your location, contacts, phone, camera, microphone, storage, or any other sensitive device features.

12. Data Retention

All data is stored locally on your device for as long as the app is installed. Data is automatically and permanently deleted when you:

  • Uninstall the app
  • Clear the app's data via Android Settings
  • Remove individual items within the app (playlists, network sources, etc.)

We retain no data on any server, as we never receive any data from your device.

13. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We will not reduce your rights under this policy without your explicit consent. For significant changes, we will provide notice through an app update. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Supervisory Authority

If you are in the EEA and believe your data protection rights have been violated, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority (DPA). However, since Ply3rs does not collect or process personal data, it is unlikely that any violation could occur.

15. Contact

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this privacy policy or your data, please contact us:

Email: ply3rs.app@gmail.com

We will respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 30 days, as required by GDPR.
